🎯 Zero-Shot Prompting

Direct instruction without examples - let the AI use its training knowledge to create your vision.

✅ Good Example
Professional corporate headshot of confident female CEO in navy blue business suit, standing in modern glass office with city skyline background, shot with 85mm portrait lens, studio lighting setup, commercial photography quality, crystal clear focus, realistic skin texture, magazine cover quality
❌ Poor Example
businesswoman
🎯 Best Practices
  • Be specific about style, quality, and details
  • Include technical terms (cinematic, professional, detailed)
  • Specify lighting, composition, and artistic style
  • Use clear, descriptive language

📚 Few-Shot Learning

Provide 2-5 examples to show the pattern you want the AI to follow.

✅ Good Example
Examples: 1. Professional surgeon: Confident female surgeon in blue scrubs, standing in modern operating room with medical equipment 2. Corporate executive: Distinguished businessman in navy suit, sitting at glass desk in downtown office with city view 3. Master chef: Experienced chef in white uniform, working in professional kitchen with stainless steel surfaces Following this pattern, create: [Your professional portrait request]
❌ Poor Example
Doctors are important. Business people are successful. Make a photographer.
🎯 Best Practices
  • Use 2-5 consistent, high-quality examples
  • Maintain consistent style and structure
  • Show the exact pattern you want replicated
  • End with clear instruction for the new task

🔗 Chain-of-Thought Reasoning

Break down the creative process into logical steps for complex artwork.

✅ Good Example
Let's create this step by step: Step 1: Choose subject - Professional female surgeon with confident expression Step 2: Define setting - Modern hospital operating room with medical equipment Step 3: Establish mood - Professional, competent, focused atmosphere Step 4: Design lighting - Bright hospital lighting with overhead surgical lights Step 5: Add details - Blue scrubs, surgical mask around neck, stethoscope Step 6: Ensure quality - Professional photography, commercial grade, sharp focus Result: Create a professional female surgeon...
❌ Poor Example
I want a doctor. Make them look professional. Add some medical stuff.
🎯 Best Practices
  • Break complex requests into clear steps
  • Show your reasoning process explicitly
  • Build systematically from basic to detailed
  • Synthesize all elements in final instruction

🎭 Role Playing

Assign a specific role or perspective to guide the AI's creative approach.

✅ Good Example
You are a master concept artist for blockbuster fantasy films. Create a piece that would impress directors and producers: A breathtaking landscape showing an ancient floating city among the clouds, with intricate architecture and dramatic lighting that conveys both majesty and mystery.
❌ Poor Example
You are an artist. Draw something nice.
🎯 Best Practices
  • Choose specific, relevant professional roles
  • Set clear context and expectations
  • Include industry-specific terminology
  • Specify the target audience or purpose

📋 Structured Prompting

Use clear sections and systematic formatting for maximum control and consistency.

✅ Good Example
SUBJECT: Professional female surgeon with confident expression STYLE: Medical documentary photography, commercial grade COMPOSITION: Portrait, direct eye contact, professional headshot framing LIGHTING: Bright hospital lighting, professional studio setup MOOD: Professional, competent, focused atmosphere DETAILS: Blue scrubs, surgical mask around neck, stethoscope TECHNICAL: Shot with 85mm lens, shallow depth of field, magazine quality
❌ Poor Example
Make a doctor. They should look professional and medical.
🎯 Best Practices
  • Use clear section headers (SUBJECT:, STYLE:, etc.)
  • Be systematic and comprehensive
  • Include technical specifications
  • Maintain consistent formatting throughout
